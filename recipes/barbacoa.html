Barbacoa Tacos

The best Barbacoa Beef recipe!

This flavorful meat is deliciously seasoned and cooked low and slow until perfectly tender. Layer it in tortillas with all your favorite toppings for a crave-worthy dinner!

Ingredients

  • Chuck roast
  • Vegetable oil
  • Beef broth
  • Chipotle chilis
  • Garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cumin, cloves, oregano, bay leaves
  • Fresh lime juice

Steps

  1. Portion beef: Cut roast into 6 portions while removing any large pieces of fat.
  2. Brown portions: Heat oil in skillet and sear seasoned beef, half at a time, until browned on all sides.
  3. Make chili paste: In a food processor, pulse together chipotle chilies, garlic and 1/4 cup beef broth until well pureed.
  4. Make barbacoa sauce: Whisk together remaining beef broth with chipotle mixture, cumin, oregano, salt, pepper and cloves.
  5. Add beef and mixtures to slow cooker: Pour mixture over beef in slow cooker, add bay leaves.
  6. Cook in Crockpot low and slow: Cover and cook on low heat 8 – 9 hours.
  7. Shred beef: Remove beef from slow cooker (leave broth) and shred.
  8. Mix in lime and rest to soak flavors: Stir lime juice into broth in slow cooker then return beef to slow cooker and cook on low or warm 20 – 30 minutes longer.
